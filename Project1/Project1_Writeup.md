# Project 1 - Imaging and Analyzing for Deleted Emails

The main goal of this project is to get experience using digital forensic tools, specifically FTK Imager and Autopsy. I have used both of these before, but I would like to do it in a documented manner. The cool thing about these projects is I can create my own scenario. With that being said, here is the first scenario:

## Duncan Dealing Drugs
Daphne noticed that her 16 year old son, Duncan, had been a little distant from the family lately. He would lock himself in his room until dinner time. After dinner, he would choose to hangout with his friends, not returning home until almost 1am. Concerned about her son's activities, Daphne decided she would go snooping through Duncan's laptop to see if she could find out what he has been up to and why her little boy no longer wants to be around the family. Unsure where to start, she decided to look through his folder of photos. There was another folder within the camera roll that said "Business Secrets." She clicks on the folder and sees dozens of pictures of different drugs. In shock, her first instinct is to call the police to report her findings, concerned that her son is going down the wrong path. Suddenly, Duncan walks into the room. Seeing that his mom had gone through his computer and is now on the phone with authorities, Duncan snatches the computer away from his mom.

As a digital forensic investigator, you have just been handed a warrant for Duncan's laptop and it is your job to find the pictures and any other media linked to drug sales/trafficking.
